About Trip NestAI

Trip NestAI is a revolutionary AI-powered travel planning platform designed for Indian explorers. What sets us apart is our fully customizable trip builder. Users can enter their travel budget, preferences, destination, and desired trip dates — and our AI crafts a personalized itinerary just for them.

🧠 Powered by smart algorithms that learn your style
💸 Budget-first: Enter your price and we’ll build your trip
🗺️ Explore 10+ top Indian cities with real insights
🛠️ Fully customizable: modify hotels, activities, or dates anytime

Our goal is simple — to give everyone the power to explore India in a way that’s affordable, unique, and truly personal.
